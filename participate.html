Registration process

The registration process is very quick and easy, it will only take you a few minutes. Two ways are possible, read below what are the steps to follow.



You can subscribe to Jogl. JOGL is a non profit headquarted in Paris, France. JOGL is a decentralized open research and innovation laboratory. You can join the program page to form and/or join a team, get access to all ressources (data sets, collaborative tools, etc).



You can subscribe directly to Dataiku(r) to get access access to data and the collaborative tool).
